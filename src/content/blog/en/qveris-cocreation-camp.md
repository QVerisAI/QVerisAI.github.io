**Recently, many people have been talking about AI Agents.**

But when it comes to real-world implementation, one question comes up again and again:

When AI wants to “actually get things done,”

**how should it choose tools and APIs, then call them reliably, safely, and at low cost?**

This is exactly the problem QVeris is working to solve.

## What Is QVeris Building?

QVeris is an **action infrastructure layer for AI Agents**.

We focus on whether **AI can truly complete a full, valuable data/tool invocation loop**:

- Access diverse real-world data and tools
- Execute reliably in production environments
- Keep costs controllable and operations auditable

If you are building AI applications, Agents, or automation systems, you have probably already run into these problems.

## Why Launch the QVeris AI Co-Creation Camp?

First, we want to make one thing clear:

> **This is not a competition, nor is it a showcase-style hackathon.**

QVeris is still in its **internal testing and capability refinement stage**.

Instead of pursuing large-scale usage right away, we want to work through **real scenarios with builders**,

**helping developers implement and continuously optimize practical use cases quickly and at low cost.**

That is why we are launching the QVeris AI Co-Creation Camp, a phased internal testing program.

## Who Is the Co-Creation Camp For?

If you:

- Are building or planning to build an **AI Agent or AI application**
- Need to call multiple APIs, tools, or data sources
- Care about development efficiency, reliability, and cost
- Want to take part in the early co-creation process of an infrastructure product

Then you are **a very strong fit!**

## What Will Happen During the Camp?

During the **2–3 week** co-creation cycle:

1. You will build a **real application scenario** based on QVeris
1. **You will work one-on-one with the QVeris team in deep co-creation**
1. **Together, we will discuss and design:**

How tools and APIs should be called

Agent execution and orchestration flows

Reliability, cost, and reusable capabilities

Any part that feels hard to implement is welcome to be raised directly 👉 we will optimize alongside you.

## What Can QVeris Provide?

1. **QVeris Token/API access + technical support**
- Priority real-time support and fast feedback on engineering iterations
- Tokens and tool invocation quota needed during the co-creation period
1. **Cash rewards for co-creation, with clear trigger conditions**

To respect real engineering effort, we have set up **small but definite cash rewards**:

- Complete a runnable demo: **¥300 – ¥500**
- Scenario capability adopted and displayed on the QVerisAI official website / jointly open-sourced: **¥500 – ¥1,000**
- **Strong promotion support: additional reward + joint promotion + deeper follow-up collaboration**
1. **Official long-term promotion and partnership opportunities**
- Co-creation projects may be included in official QVeris case studies (optional)
- Long-term token support plans and a dedicated engineering feedback channel

## **How Participation Works**

- The Co-Creation Camp will run in a **small-scale, phased format**
- We will prioritize projects with **real scenarios and a willingness to keep investing effort**
- As our capabilities mature, we will continue opening up more co-creation and internal testing opportunities to more developers
