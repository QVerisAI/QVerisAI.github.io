## **Start Taking Action, Agent!**
**Goal**: Build and run a truly executable Agent action loop within 4 hours.

(Successfully call at least 1 real tool API through Qveris)

**Prizes**: 3 awards, each worth RMB 2,000 (RMB 500 cash + RMB 1,500 equivalent in QverisAI Credits)

**Time**: Sunday, 13:30–20:00

**Location**: 9F, Tower A, Dongsheng Building, Wudaokou, Haidian District, Beijing (Origin Academy)

**Check-in**: 13:30

**Format**: Offline co-creation hackathon (free team formation / solo participation)

**Scale**: 30–50 people (space is limited; thank you for your understanding)

**Rules & Demo Standards**
No restrictions on direction, industry, or product format.

We only care about one thing:

👉 Whether you can build and run a truly executable Agent action loop within 4 hours

The final product can be previewed and presented locally on your own computer.

**3 Projects Will Be Selected**
**(One team per award: ¥2,000 each, RMB 500 cash + RMB 1,500 equivalent in Qveris credits)**

1.The Best PMF: Project with the strongest commercial potential (1 team, on-site voting)

2.Just for Fun: Most fun project (1 team)

3.Qveris Special Award: Project that uncovers the most value from Qveris (1 team)

**Judging Criteria:**

A project is considered complete and eligible for judging once it is submitted to GitHub.
