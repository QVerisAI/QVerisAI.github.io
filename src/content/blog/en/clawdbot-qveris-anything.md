## What is Clawdbot?

Clawdbot is an open-source AI assistant that can be installed on your device and interact with users through channels such as WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord. It can remember long-term context and execute a range of automated tasks, including email management, calendar reminders, file operations, and even user-specified scripts and browser actions.

## Clawdbot’s rise points to a long-standing pain point

Over the past few years, large language models have advanced quickly. But the way people use AI has barely changed:

A person asks a question. The AI answers.

This is an **extremely passive** interaction model.

Clawdbot is compelling because **it moves AI into a state of continuous action.**

Your AI no longer waits for you to click on it. It begins to **actively exist** across the social platforms where you already spend time, including X, Discord, and Telegram.

But that also introduces a new problem.

Clawdbot solves the problem of AI agency, but it still faces the same issue that has affected large language models for years: **it cannot call massive amounts of real, specific external data sources. In fields such as finance, investing, and business analysis, uncertain data can be more dangerous than no data at all.**

QVeris is built to fill exactly that gap.
